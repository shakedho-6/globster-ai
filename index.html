Globster.ai is a hosted platform that lets anyone deploy a personal AI agent in under 2 minutes. Not a chatbot. Not a copilot. An operational hire — with its own name, personality, email address, phone number, and credit card. It connects to your tools, lives on your favorite channels, and gets smarter every day it works with you. Built by monday agent labs. Powered by NVIDIA NemoClaw. Managed by monday.com.
ChatGPT answers questions. Globster executes tasks. Most AI tools reset every session — they have no memory, no tools, and no presence in the world. Your Globster agent remembers everything across every conversation and every channel. It has a real-world identity — its own email, phone, and credit card — and can act on your behalf autonomously. It's the difference between an AI you talk to and an AI that works for you.

Each integration you add is a new capability your agent can use from day one. The library is growing every month. You can also connect any tool with an API or MCP directly to your Globster agent.
