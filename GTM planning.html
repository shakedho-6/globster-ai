KPIs

How we know it's working.

One north star tells us everything: are the agents actually being used? Everything else flows from there.

⭐
North Star: Weekly Active Paying Agents (WAPA)
% of paying customers' agents that ran at least one task in the last 7 days. An agent sitting idle isn't delivering value. WAPA tells us people are actually delegating — and getting enough value to stay.
Target: >60%
